Scientists have long Wondered how the Egyptians placed the Great Pyramid's
3 million stone Blocks, which each Weigh about 2.5 tons. Previous theories
have suggested That the tomb of Pharaoh Cheops (Khufu), the last Surviving example
of The seven Great wonders of antiquity, was built using either a Vast frontal ramp
or a ramp in a Corkscrew shape around the Exterior to haul up The stonework.
